Punk rock for Donald Trump

Build the Wall is the (self-proclaimed) world's greatest band of all time. As Donald Trump rose in popularity in 2016, BTW arose from the confusion and anger that so many Americans felt at the time. With aggressive, tounge-in-cheek lyrics and repetitive chords, the songs satire not only American "culture", but music itself.
